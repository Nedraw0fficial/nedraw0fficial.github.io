THE EIGHT WORLDS

So we've got eight planets in our solar system, and they're all pretty different from each other. Mercury's close to the Sun and moves fast, Neptune's way out there and freezing cold. Each one's got its own thing going on. Here's the rundown:

PLAYER SELECT: THE PLANETS

  • MERCURY - SPEED: ★★★★★ | SIZE: ★☆☆☆☆
    The smallest and fastest planet. Mercury is the speed runner of the solar system, orbiting the Sun in just 88 days. Its surface is covered in craters and experiences extreme temperature swings. Special move: Temperature Flux Attack!
  • VENUS - DEFENSE: ★★★★★ | HEAT: ★★★★★
    Earth's twisted sister with a thick toxic atmosphere and surface hot enough to melt lead. Venus has clouds made of sulfuric acid and spins backwards. Special ability: Acid Cloud Shield!
  • EARTH - LIFE: ★★★★★ | WATER: ★★★★★
    Home base! The only planet confirmed to support life. With liquid water, breathable atmosphere, and diverse ecosystems, Earth is the ultimate life support system. Special power: Biosphere Generation!
  • MARS - EXPLORATION: ★★★★☆ | TERRAIN: ★★★★☆
    The Red Planet and humanity's next target. Mars has the largest volcano in the solar system and evidence of ancient water. Currently being explored by multiple rovers. Boss level unlocked!
  • JUPITER - SIZE: ★★★★★ | POWER: ★★★★★
    The absolute unit of the solar system! Jupiter is so massive it could fit all other planets inside it. Its Great Red Spot is a storm bigger than Earth that's been raging for centuries. Ultimate ability: Gravity Well!
  • SATURN - STYLE: ★★★★★ | RINGS: ★★★★★
    The Lord of the Rings (literally). Saturn's stunning ring system is made of ice and rock particles. It has over 80 moons, including Titan with its thick atmosphere. Fashion stat: MAXED!
  • URANUS - TILT: ★★★★★ | ICE: ★★★★☆
    The sideways planet that rotates on its side, possibly due to a massive ancient collision. This ice giant has a blue-green color from methane in its atmosphere. Special quirk: Horizontal Spin!
  • NEPTUNE - WIND: ★★★★★ | DISTANCE: ★★★★★
    The final boss of the solar system! Neptune has the fastest winds in the solar system at 1,200 mph. Its deep blue color and dynamic atmosphere make it one of the most mysterious planets. Ultimate stage!

BONUS LEVELS: DWARF PLANETS

Past Neptune, there's a bunch of dwarf planets floating around - Pluto's the famous one, but there's also Eris, Ceres, and others. They're smaller than regular planets but still interesting. Out there you've also got the Kuiper Belt and Oort Cloud, which are basically huge areas full of icy rocks and comets that we're still learning about.
