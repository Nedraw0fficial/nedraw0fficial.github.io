WELCOME PLAYER ONE

So yeah, basically this is a space exploration site but with that old-school arcade vibe from the 80s. Think neon lights, chunky pixels, and all that retro stuff mixed with actual space facts. Pretty cool combo if you ask me.

GAME FEATURES

I put together a bunch of stuff about space missions and planets, threw in some info about the tech we use to get out there, and yeah - there's even a (glitched) Space Invaders game you can actually play. Figured it fit the theme quite well 'innit ?. (Took me so long lmao)

Space exploration is honestly wild when you think about it. We started with just sending beeping satellites into orbit, and now we've got rovers rolling around on Mars. Each mission was kind of like beating a level and unlocking the next one.
