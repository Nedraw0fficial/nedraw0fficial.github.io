THE APOLLO LEGACY

The Apollo program was like the ultimate boss level of space exploration. Between 1969 and 1972, twelve astronauts walked on the Moon, collecting samples, conducting experiments, and proving that humanity could reach beyond our planet. It was the greatest multiplayer co-op mission ever attempted!

The tech innovations from Apollo were game-changing. Computer systems, communications, life support - all these got massive upgrades that still influence how we design spacecraft today. It's like the original developers created the foundation for all future space missions.

NEXT LEVEL: ARTEMIS

Today, NASA's Artemis program is loading the next chapter. The goal? Return humans to the Moon and eventually reach Mars. It's like a New Game+ mode with better graphics, improved AI, and expanded multiplayer features. The game isn't over - we're just getting started!
