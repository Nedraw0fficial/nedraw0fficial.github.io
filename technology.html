TECH TREE: EARTHSIDE UPGRADES

Many technologies developed for space have spawned amazing upgrades for Earth! Memory foam was originally created for spacecraft seats - now it's in mattresses and medical equipment everywhere. Water purification systems designed for the International Space Station help provide clean water in remote locations. GPS, weather satellites, and global communications are all side quests that became essential to daily life.

Looking ahead, the tech being developed for Mars colonization is going to be game-changing. Advanced 3D printing can build structures from local materials, closed-loop life support systems recycle everything, and in-situ resource utilization means creating fuel and oxygen from Martian resources. These innovations will level up life on Earth too, helping with sustainable construction, resource conservation, and living in extreme environments.

CHEAT CODES FROM SPACE

Space exploration has given us some incredible "cheat codes" for everyday technology. Satellite imagery helps farmers optimize crops, disaster response teams coordinate rescues, and scientists track climate change. Miniaturized electronics from spacecraft made smartphones possible. Even the technology in your pocket has space DNA - thank you, NASA!
